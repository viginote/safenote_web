Privacy Principles

SafeNote is designed as a community awareness platform. The goal is to minimise unnecessary personal information while allowing communities to understand local incident trends.

Important: This page is a working privacy principles page, not final legal advice. Before public launch, SafeNote should have a POPIA-reviewed Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

What SafeNote is designed to collect

  • Incident type
  • Severity
  • Approximate location or GPS coordinates
  • Timestamp
  • Optional short note

What public reports should not require

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • ID numbers
  • Public user profiles

Public map delay

The public map is designed to show delayed information for community awareness. Real-time access is reserved for verified NHW/CPF structures.

NHW / CPF access

Verified community structures may receive access to additional dashboards, live feeds and analytics for planning and reporting purposes.

Emergency disclaimer

SafeNote is not an emergency response service. For emergencies, users must contact SAPS, medical emergency services, fire services, armed response providers or other official channels.
