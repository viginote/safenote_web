Terms of Use

Rules and limitations for using SafeNote as a community awareness platform.

Important: These documents are operational legal templates for the SafeNote platform and should be reviewed by a South African legal professional before full public rollout.

By accessing or using SafeNote, you agree to these Terms of Use. If you do not agree, do not use the platform.

2. Awareness Platform Only

SafeNote provides community awareness and incident logging tools only. It does not provide emergency response, policing, security dispatch, armed response, legal advice, investigation services, prosecution services or guaranteed protection.

3. Emergencies

For emergencies, users must contact SAPS, medical emergency services, fire services, armed response providers, municipal emergency services or other official channels. SafeNote must not be relied on as an emergency reporting or response mechanism.

4. User-Generated Information

Reports submitted through SafeNote are user-generated. SafeNote does not guarantee that any report, map point, heatmap, statistic or trend is complete, verified, accurate, current or suitable for decision-making.

5. No Guarantee of Safety

SafeNote does not guarantee that any route, suburb, street, building, public area or private area is safe or unsafe. Users remain responsible for their own decisions, movements and safety precautions.

6. No Vigilantism or Retaliation

SafeNote may not be used to organise, encourage or justify vigilantism, harassment, intimidation, retaliation, threats, unlawful surveillance or unlawful confrontation.

7. Prohibited Conduct

  • Submitting false, malicious, misleading or exaggerated reports
  • Targeting individuals, households, businesses or groups without lawful basis
  • Publishing personal information or identifying details
  • Using information to commit, plan or facilitate crime
  • Using SafeNote to harass, threaten, defame or intimidate others
  • Attempting to bypass access controls, scrape data or exploit the platform

8. Bad Actors and Misuse Warning

Any person attempting to exploit SafeNote information to commit crime, evade law enforcement, interfere with community safety structures, mislead the public, sabotage the platform or misuse restricted access may have their access revoked and may be reported to relevant authorities where appropriate.

9. NHW / CPF Access

Access codes are issued for legitimate community safety structures only. Codes may not be shared publicly, sold, published, transferred or used outside their authorised purpose. SafeNote may revoke access without notice if misuse is suspected.

11. Platform Changes

SafeNote may update, restrict, suspend, remove or modify features, access, reports, data, exports and user permissions at any time for safety, security, legal, operational or abuse-prevention reasons.

12. Governing Law

These terms are intended for use in South Africa and should be reviewed for compliance with applicable South African laws before full public launch.
