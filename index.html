Simple by design

One clear purpose.

SafeNote is not an emergency response service. It is a community awareness platform designed to help people understand what is happening around them.

1

Report

Residents anonymously report incidents occurring in their area without creating public profiles or sharing contact details.

2

Map

Reports are organised by incident type, time and area to create a clearer picture of community activity.

3

Understand

Verified community structures can view trends, heatmaps and summaries to support awareness and patrol planning.

Built for local structures

Made for South African communities.

Designed around the way communities already work: residents, patrollers, Neighbourhood Watches, CPF structures and local safety groups.

Privacy-first

Designed to reduce unnecessary personal data.

No names required for public incident reports.
No public user profiles or reporter identities.
Public data is delayed and designed for awareness, not response.
Verified NHW/CPF access is separated from the public map.

Clear boundaries matter.

SafeNote does not replace SAPS, emergency services, armed response or official reporting channels. It helps communities create a shared picture of activity so local structures can make better-informed decisions.

Community access

One simple place for community awareness.
